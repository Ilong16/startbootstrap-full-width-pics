Orion Corperation

The Hunter of the new Frontier

Orion Corperation was founded in 1998 by Isaac Long (43). It was named after the hunter in greek mythology "Orion" and the star constellation named after the same mytholigical character. Orion Corperation was origonally a research based organisation that would use data recieved by NASA in order to publish scientific studies. In recent years we have turned into our own space agency and will be launching our first orbital vessel in 2020. Orion also currently has contracts with the United States and the United Kingdom for aircraft manufacturing.

Saiph 01

Suborbital Aircraft

The Atlas is Orion Corperation's first experimental aircraft supplied to the United States and United Kingdom. It's top speed is a little over mach 3 at exactly 2,307.9 miles per hour without an armed payload. The first model was manufactured in 2017 and is still being produced.
